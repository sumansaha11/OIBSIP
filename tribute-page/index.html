Biography

Tony Stark, also known as Iron Man, is a fictional character from Marvel Comics. Tony Stark was a billionaire industrialist and genius inventor who heads Stark Industries. After suffering a severe chest injury during a kidnapping, Stark build a powered suit of armor to save his life and escape captivity. He later refines the suit to protect the world as Iron Man. Stark was known for his quick wit, flamboyant personality, and complex character arc, which explores themes of redemption, responsibility, and the impact of advanced technology on society. During the final battle - The Endgame, he uses the Infinity Stones to erase Thanos and his army, knowing it will be fatal. The immense power of the stones was too much for him to withstand, and he dies surrounded by his friends, with his last words being,

"I AM IRON MAN."
